Where We Work

We are actively engaged across the African continent, with our primary office located in Nairobi, Kenya. Our work spans various sectors, and we collaborate with a wide range of clients, including international NGOs, national governments, regional organizations, bilateral and multilateral donors, private corporations, and philanthropic organizations.

Since our inception, we have provided expert technical assistance in the implementation of over 120 donor-funded projects. These projects focus on key areas such as health, education, livelihoods, markets, and capacity development. With a particular focus on Kenya and East Africa, we are committed to delivering impactful, sustainable solutions that address local challenges while contributing to broader regional development goals.
